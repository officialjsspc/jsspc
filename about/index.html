About Us

JSSATEN Photography Club is a society built up of students belonging to different departments which intends to make its members learn and be well versed in the fields of photography, social media management and data handling. It aspires to promote the practical aspect of photography by executing activities such as photo-walks, competitions and workshops.Founded in 2018, club nurtures the talent of film and photography enthusiasts of the college.

Vision

The Vision of our Club is is to make its members learn and be well versed in the fields of Photography, Videography, social media management and data handling and to promote the practical aspect of Photography & Videography by executing activities such as photo-walks, competitions, workshops etc.
