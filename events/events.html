Photowalk 2021

JSS Photography Club organized a Photo walk at Lodhi Garden and Yamuna Ghat in Delhi. Photo walk aims to unleash creativity within budding photographers who try to explore new places and to click creative pictures. Students were accompanied by Virat Vidhut, President and Ritik Kumar, CTC. They taught the basic rules of the Photography and let the students click various pictures.
This event gave the beginners an opportunity to learn the functionalities of DSLR practically and interact with other fellow members.
